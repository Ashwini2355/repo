My Dearest Varshit

I realized how much I could rely on you not just for practical support, but for the way you made me feel safe, understood, and cared for which I have always craved throughout my life. There was this comfort in knowing I had someone I could trust, someone who won't think twice to stand for me. There was this effortless understanding between us, and it felt so natural to express myself in a way I had struggled to do before with all my friends just to make them understand what I actually mean. You just get me, and I can tell you things I’ve thought ten times to share with anyone.

What amazed me the most was how much I looked forward to our daily conversations. It felt like I was growing closer to you with every exchange, learning more about your life, your experiences, and your dreams. You made me feel so comfortable, not just in listening to your stories, but in sharing my own as well. There was something so soothing about our time together, and I found myself eagerly waiting for our next conversation.
